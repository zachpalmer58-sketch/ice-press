Marketing & Product Vision

Hello App Store Verification Team and valued users! I am ZACH PALMER, an enthusiastic indie developer putting deep effort, dedication, and meticulous care into building Ice-Press.

Current Monetization Status: Ice-Press is entirely free of charge at launch. There are absolutely no hidden microtransactions, active subscriptions, or paywalls integrated yet.

Future Sustainability Roadmap: To ensure long-term maintenance and active expansion of features, I plan to integrate premium tools and optional subscription models in later updates. This will establish a clear framework to support independent development and drive high product value.

My ultimate focus is to provide maximum immediate utility, fluid design language, and optimized responsiveness for a stunning user experience.
